Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new features will be immediately available. If you downgrade, the changes will apply to your next billing cycle.

What happens when I reach my monthly limits?

When you reach your monthly AI completion or image generation limits, you have two options: wait until your limits reset the next month, or upgrade to a higher tier plan for immediate access to higher limits.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of our Pro plan, with no credit card required. You'll get full access to all Pro features so you can evaluate if it meets your needs.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team within 30 days of your initial purchase for a full refund.

Do you offer custom enterprise plans?

Yes, we offer tailored enterprise solutions for organizations with specific requirements. Contact our sales team to discuss your needs and get a custom quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and for enterprise customers, we can arrange invoice-based payments.
